Discover the Magic of Detroit:

A City of Icons and Innovations

Explore the heart of Detroit, a city that offers an array of astonishing sights and experiences, whether you're visiting for the first time or rediscovering your hometown. Detroit's rich tapestry of history, groundbreaking architecture, and vibrant cultural spots - including the majestic Fisher Building, the historic Fox Theater, and the legendary Motown Museum - are just the beginning of what this city has in store.

Immerse yourself in the beauty of Belle Isle, relive the excitement of major events like the Super Bowl and Final Four, and uncover the stories behind Detroit's remarkable contribution to music. From Motown's soulful beats to techno's electronic rhythms, Detroit's musical legacy has left an indelible mark on the world, influencing genres across the board.

But the best way to experience the depth and breadth of Detroit's offerings is through our guided tours. Led by our multilingual, knowledgeable guides, you'll dive deep into the heart of the city's fascinating history, iconic architecture, and the people and places that make it truly unique. Offering tours in languages including Russian, German, Mandarin, Cantonese, and more, we ensure that your journey through Detroit is as informative as it is enchanting.

Join us and uncover the myriad wonders of Detroit. With stories to tell and secrets to reveal, our tours offer a window into the soul of a city that continues to inspire and amaze. Discover Detroit with us, and see why it's a city worth exploring time and time again.

Experience the heart of Detroit in a captivating 2-hour walking tour

Starting at the breathtaking Guardian Building, an Art Deco masterpiece and National Historic Landmark. Wander through downtown Detroit, uncovering its rich history and stunning architecture, from the lively Hart Plaza on the riverfront, home to spectacular events like the Detroit Electronic Music Festival, to the acclaimed Detroit International Riverfront, voted the best river walk in the U.S. Marvel at Campus Martius Park, hailed as the top public square by USA Today, and discover hidden gems of unique sculptures, buildings, local art galleries, and eateries that only insiders know about.
